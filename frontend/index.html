About NCC

Nashik Detachment

Unit: 01 MAH AIR SQN NCC, MUMBAI (NASHIK DETACHMENT)

Group: MUMBAI B GROUP, MAHARASHTRA DIRECTORATE

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is the world's largest uniformed youth organization. Our mission is to develop character, discipline, leadership, and secular outlook in the youth of India.

Commanding Officer: Dr. Thakur Akash Ashok

Under the visionary leadership of Dr. Thakur Akash Ashok, the Nashik Detachment continues to inspire and train cadets to become exemplary citizens with a spirit of service and patriotism.
